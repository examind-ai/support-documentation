# Student Direct Pay Setup

Student Direct Pay lets students purchase access to an EXAMIND course directly by credit card.

## Who can enable Student Direct Pay

Student Direct Pay is enabled by the EXAMIND team.

To request setup, the instructor must email support@examind.io.

## How Student Direct Pay works

1. A student enters the course in EXAMIND.
2. If payment is required and not yet completed, the student is prompted to pay by credit card.
3. After payment, the student can continue and access course assessments.

## Pricing and purchase behavior

- Student Direct Pay is a one-time purchase per course, per student.
- Once a student has paid for that course, they do not need to pay again for that same course.

## What to tell students

Instructors should communicate:

1. Payment is required to access the course in EXAMIND.
2. Payment is by credit card.
3. Purchase is one-time per course.

## Need help

For setup or support, contact support@examind.io.
