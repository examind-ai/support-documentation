# Post Grades

## Accessing EXAMIND LTI Page

1. Navigate to assessment in your LMS that is linked to EXAMIND.&#x20;
2. Click on EXAMIND assessment to load EXAMIND LTI page&#x20;

## Post Grades

1. Click on **Post Grades**
2. Verify grades have been imported into your LMS.

{% hint style="info" %}
Grades are not posted automatically to your LMS upon a student's submission.
{% endhint %}
