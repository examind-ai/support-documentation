# EXAMIND Platform

The EXAMIND Platform is the core web app for building, delivering, and auto-grading assessments — both quantitative work (calculations, journal entries, Excel models) and qualitative work (essays, case studies, memos) — with academic integrity built in. You can use it on its own or integrated with your LMS.

It follows a simple lifecycle:

* [**Get Started**](get-started/README.md) — set up EXAMIND on its own or connected to your LMS.
* [**Build**](build/README.md) — create questions (eight types, with dynamic per-student variants) and assemble them into assessments.
* [**Deliver**](deliver/README.md) — schedule, monitor, and protect the integrity of assessments.
* [**Grade**](grade/README.md) — review auto-graded results, make adjustments, and release self-review.

{% hint style="info" %}
For AI feedback and auto-grading of written work, see [Feedback Machines](feedback-machines/get-started.md) — a separate, complementary product.
{% endhint %}
