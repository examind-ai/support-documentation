# EXAMIND Platform

The EXAMIND Platform is the core web app for building, delivering, and auto-grading assessments — both quantitative work (calculations, journal entries, Excel models) and qualitative work (essays, case studies, memos) — with academic integrity built in. Use it on its own or integrated with your LMS.

## What you can do with it

* **Many question types, one reusable bank** — build with [eight question types](build/question-builder/question-types/README.md) and organize them in a [question bank](build/question-bank/README.md) you can reuse and share across courses.
* **A unique assessment for every student** — the [dynamic question engine](build/question-builder/dynamic-questions/README.md) generates per-student variants of a question, so no two students see exactly the same exam.
* **Beyond multiple choice** — assess applied skills with [Excel Assignments](deliver/excel-assignments/README.md), open-response [Essay assessments](build/assessments/essay-assessments.md) (with an optional AI writing assistant), and AI-driven [Simulations](build/assessments/simulation-assessments.md).
* **Integrity built in** — monitor attempts live with [Live View](deliver/live-view.md), discourage misconduct with [Tactical Deterrents](deliver/tactical-deterrents.md), control [offline behavior](deliver/offline-behavior.md), and connect your [proctoring tool](it-administration/proctoring-integrations/README.md).
* **Grading that mostly does itself** — most question types are [auto-graded](grade/README.md) the moment students submit, with [analytics](grade/results.md), [grading adjustments](grade/grading-adjustment.md), and student [self-review](grade/self-review.md).
* **Standalone or in your LMS** — use EXAMIND on its own, or [integrate via LTI](it-administration/lms-integrations/README.md) with Canvas, Blackboard, Brightspace, or Moodle for roster sync, deep-linking, and grade passback.
