# Review & Export Results

From a Feedback Machine you can review every submission, grade a whole class at once, and export results to your Canvas gradebook.

## Review submissions

Open a machine and select **Submissions** to see every draft, with the student's email, the time submitted, and the score. Select any submission to read its full feedback.

## Grade a whole class at once

For a class-scoped machine, **Bulk Import** lets you upload many student submissions together (for example, from a spreadsheet). Feedback Machines evaluates them all against your rubric so you don't have to collect drafts one at a time.

## Export to your gradebook

Use **Bulk Export** to download results in the format you need:

* **Canvas gradebook** — scores as a CSV you can import into Canvas.
* **Feedback files** — a zip of per-student feedback.
* **Submissions** — a zip of the original student files.

You can export all students, a specific import, or only each student's highest-scoring submission.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Adjusting a machine:** You can **Modify** a machine's assignment or rubric, or change its **Settings** (visibility, sharing, submission type) at any time. Changes apply to *future* submissions — existing results aren't re-graded.
{% endhint %}
