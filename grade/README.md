# Grade

EXAMIND auto-grades submissions as they come in, so grading is mostly about **reviewing and refining**: check the results and analytics, make any adjustments you need, and decide what students get to see afterward.

## In this section

* [**Results**](results.md) — review auto-graded scores with analysis views (score distribution, by student, by question).
* [**Grading Adjustment**](grading-adjustment.md) — override individual scores, omit questions, or give full credit.
* [**Self Review**](self-review.md) — open a post-assessment window where students can see their results, answer keys, and explanations.
