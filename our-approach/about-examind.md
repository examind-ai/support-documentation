# About EXAMIND

## Our mission

To build learning tools for educators that improve student outcomes and engagement in an efficient and effective way.

## Our vision

To help educators create students who become lifelong learners and contributors to a better society.

## Our values

We summarize our core values in the acronym **EXAM**, inspired by our name:

* **Education** — we believe in being lifelong learners and inspiring others to continuously grow and develop new knowledge and skills.
* **'Xperience** — we put the human experience at the center of everything we do: simple, accessible, and user-friendly, even when that means more work for us.
* **Authenticity** — we believe everyone should live the life they want to live, and we support each other in being who we are, in life and at work.
* **Movement** — we believe progress over perfection: building habits and processes that let us improve the instructor and student experience faster.
